TEDx UofW: University of Washington TEDx Event 2025

Welcome to the information page about TEDx UofW, the independently organized TED event at the University of Washington in Seattle.

About TEDx UofW

TEDx UofW is an independently organized TED event that takes place at the University of Washington in Seattle. The "x" in TEDx indicates an independently organized TED event, bringing the spirit of TED's mission of "ideas worth spreading" to local communities around the globe.

For 2025, we present TEDx UofW: Resonance, an event focused on ideas that resonate across disciplines, cultures, and experiences.

University of Washington TEDx History

The University of Washington TEDx event has a rich history of bringing together innovative thinkers, creators, and visionaries from the Pacific Northwest and beyond. Our mission is to create a platform for "ideas worth spreading" within the UW community.

TEDx 2025 Event Information

The upcoming TEDx 2025 at UW will feature a carefully curated lineup of speakers addressing this year's theme of "Resonance." Join us for an inspiring day of ideas, connection, and innovation.

TEDx UofW Team

The TEDx UofW team consists of dedicated students and staff who work tirelessly to create an exceptional TEDx experience. These passionate individuals bring diverse skills and perspectives to organize the University of Washington's TEDx event.
